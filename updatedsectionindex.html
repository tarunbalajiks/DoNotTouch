About me

I’m Tarun Balaji K S, a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student with a strong interest in AI/ML and cybersecurity. My journey in tech began with building a speech recognition assistant inspired by Jarvis from Iron Man. Since then, I've developed AI models and algorithms for facial recognition and emotion analysis, and I am also a content creator, helping over 70,000 learners on Instagram.

As the founder of load_thecode and leader of several student tech communities, including NeuronNet and GDSC, my goal is to use my expertise in AI/ML to bridge the gap between academia and industry, ensuring accessible learning and innovative solutions. I strive to combine technical skills with leadership to empower others in technology.
